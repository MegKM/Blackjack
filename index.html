How to play

The goal of blackjack is simple—to get as close as possible to 21 without going over, and to have a higher hand than the dealer. If the dealer goes over 21, they bust and lose the game. The same goes for you.
Jacks, Queens & Kings are worth 10, all other cards are face value, Aces will initially be 11 unless you bust with one in your hand, then it drops to being worth 1.
To start the game, select how much you want to bet using the chips above then hit deal. You will be dealt 2 cards and be shown only one of the dealer's cards—the other will remain hidden until you have finished making your moves.
Your options are:

  • Hit – You will be dealt one more card.
  • Double – This is only available when you have 2 cards in your hand. You will double your bet, receive one more card and stand.
  • Stand – Receive no more cards, it's now the dealer's turn.
The dealer will try to beat you without going over 21. If the dealer's cards are higher than yours, you lose your bet. If your cards are higher than the dealer's cards then you will receive double your bet into your bank.

Blackjack
Blackjack occurs when you or the dealer has reached 21 with only 2 cards in your hand.
If you both receive this, the game is over and your bet is returned.
If you have Blackjack and the dealer does not then you will win 3x your bet.
Good luck!
